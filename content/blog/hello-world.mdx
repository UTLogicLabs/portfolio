Welcome to my corner of the internet.

I built this site to have a place that's entirely mine — no algorithmic feed, no character limits, no platform decisions I don't control. Just writing and work I want to share.

## What I'll write about

Mostly software: things I'm building, problems I ran into, approaches that worked better than I expected (or worse). Sometimes the craft of engineering itself — how to think about a problem, how to communicate clearly in code.

Occasionally something off-topic. We'll see.

## How this site works

Built with React Router v7, deployed to Cloudflare Pages. Blog posts are MDX files committed directly to the repo — no CMS, no database for content. Simple, versioned, fast.

More on the technical choices in a future post.
